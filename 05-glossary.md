Reference standard: **Merriam-Webster** (NORAM). This glossary overrides for Instructure-specific and regional terms.

---

## Key Brand Terms

| Term | Definition / Usage Note |
|------|------------------------|
| **artificial intelligence (AI)** | Always lowercase generic; capitalize AI abbreviation after first spell-out. "In education, AI can support personalized learning, automate routine tasks, enhance feedback, and provide data-driven insights." |
| **assessment** | Evidence-gathering to evaluate student learning. Supports instructional improvement and outcome measurement. |
| **cloud-based** | Remote server-hosted software. Hyphenated as modifier. |
| **credentialing** | Awarding verified recognition (badges, certificates) for demonstrated skills. |
| **ecosystem** | "A network of interacting people, companies, and technologies" — Canvas, Mastery, Parchment, platform enhancements, Community, and partners. |
| **edtech** | Always lowercase. Industry shorthand for education technology. |
| **learning management system (LMS)** | "A centralized digital hub" for content creation, delivery, and progress tracking. Spell out on first use. |
| **pedagogy** | "The art and science of teaching." |
| **scalability** | System capability to handle growth in users, content, data, or complexity. |
