## What It Is
A tiered integration partnership program that gives edtech companies access to Instructure's global user base, technical integration support, and co-marketing opportunities.

**Headline:** Grow your edtech solution with Instructure integration

**Subhead:** Meet educators and students where they already are and broaden your user base with flexible, tiered options to support high-quality integrations with all components of the Instructure platform.

**Short description:**
Your users use Instructure. With 8k+ customer institutions and tens of millions of educators, administrators, and learners across higher education, K12, and corporate markets in more than 100 countries, Instructure Platform Partnership offers unparalleled access to edtech users, plus the technical and promotional support necessary to drive successful adoptions and realize meaningful growth.

## The Enemy

**In a volatile market, not every edtech company can be a winner.** The edtech market is increasingly crowded, while funding sources are becoming less reliable and institution budgets are shrinking. Edtech solutions must demonstrate value now, or risk declining sales and reduced investment. Visibility, evidence, and interoperability are critical.

## Value Pillars

**1. Grow your reach in the edtech market**
Access Instructure's vast global user base through tiered partnership options.

- **8,000+ customer institutions**, tens of millions of users, 100+ countries
- Comprehensive **listings in Canvas Apps, LearnPlatform, and the Instructure Community**
- **Discounted access to Partner Summit** (market trends, early roadmap previews, networking)
- **Sponsorship opportunities** for InstructureCon and CanvasCon

Key proof: Instructure.com is the most visited education website in the world (SEMRush). 2.2M+ active educators in the Instructure Community.

**2. Leverage expert integration consulting to build R&D capacity**
Whether building a full LTI integration from scratch or overcoming a specific hurdle, Instructure's in-house consultants help build integrations that fully comply with privacy, security, and accessibility standards.

- **Integration Certification** — verify LTI 1.3 compliance, strong Canvas UX, transparent data privacy/accessibility practices. Differentiates in RFPs.
- **Custom Development Services** — contract-based engagements to level-up integration
- **Developer Relations Office Hours** — monthly access to integration experts
- **30+ LTI 1.3 placements** in Canvas; **750+ API endpoints** available

Key proof: "The accessibility, the security, all of the compliance that each individual institution burdens a young company with is insurmountable… So what Instructure has done with this partner program, by giving a young startup that initial sense of compliance…that's incredibly valuable." — Robert Peregoodoff, CLO, TimelyGrader

**3. Establish ROI in a crowded market**
Access unique research services and co-marketing benefits to clearly define the impact of your solution.

- **WWC-certified research team** — 80+ years of combined education research experience, 250+ ESSA-aligned studies delivered or validated
- **ESSA-aligned efficacy research** — meets requirements for public sector funding eligibility
- **Priority listings and thought leadership** content opportunities
