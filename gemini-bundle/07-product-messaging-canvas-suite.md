## Canvas Studio — Interactive Video Learning

### The Enemy
Learner disengagement, compounded by one-way, passive video content. Students consume video like social media — scrolling, not learning.

### Elevator Pitch
**Short (50–100 words):**
Canvas Studio is an interactive video learning platform that works natively inside the Canvas LMS. It turns any video — teacher-created, district-curated, or third-party — into an active learning moment. Educators add questions, time-stamped discussions, and checks for understanding directly in the video, then see who watched, where learners struggled, and which concepts need reteaching. Built-in accessibility, role-based permissions, and content governance protect student data and scale high-quality instruction across schools.

### Three Value Pillars

**1. Active, participatory learning**
Transform passive video into interactive experiences with in-video discussions and embedded quizzes. Counters the "6-minute drop-off" problem — students engage with content, don't just watch it.

**2. Verifiable data and insights**
See who watched, where attention dropped, which concepts need reinforcement. Feeds directly into instruction — intervene sooner, prove impact to leaders.

**3. Governance, privacy, and accessibility**
Centralized control, role-based permissions, auto-generated captions. Meets FERPA/COPPA. Replaces consumer video hosts (YouTube, Kaltura) that create security and equity gaps.

### Target Audiences
| Sector | Motion | Key Buyers |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| K-12 | Cross-sell | Chief Academic Officer, CTO, Assessment Director, Principal, Director of Instructional Technology |
| Higher Ed | Cross-sell | Deputy Vice Chancellor (APAC), Provost, CTO, Director of Online Learning, Instructional Designer |
| BizGov – Educational Services | Net-new | Chief Learning Officer, CTO, VP of Product |

### Key Proof Points
- "Average engagement time [with video] maxes out at about 6 minutes" — edX study of 6.9M sessions
- 60% of teachers reported students had more difficulty understanding lessons in virtual environments — GAO 2022
- Only 26% of edtech products meet minimum privacy safeguards — Common Sense Media
- Embedding quiz questions throughout video produces significantly higher test scores — TechTrends higher-ed study

### Competitive Framing (What Not to Say)
Do not position Studio as "video management." Competitors to acknowledge without naming directly:
- Generic video hosts (ads, tracking, no governance) 
- Kaltura/Panopto (overly complex and expensive)
- EdPuzzle (K-12 focused but falls short vs. Studio)
- Tool fragmentation (dozens of point solutions)

**Our differentiator:** Studio is not a point solution — it removes unscalable, manual workflows and keeps learning in Canvas.

### APAC Notes
- Points 3 and 4 (data visibility vs. editing) can be swapped — data is a higher priority conversation in APAC
- YouTube Player for Education is a strong selling point in Australia due to social media age restrictions for under-16s
- Replace "Districts" with region-appropriate terms in K-12 narratives

## Canvas Catalog — Non-Degree and Faculty Training

**Source:** `Brand Website Product Messaging Docs/Canvas/Canvas Catalog _ Product Messaging Doc [FINAL].md` (Dec 4, 2025)
**Owner:** Anna Lindsay (APAC: Farrah King, EMEA: João Victor Silveira)

### What It Is
Canvas Catalog unifies the administration and learning experience for non-degree programs and faculty professional development within the Canvas ecosystem. It provides an integrated storefront and automated enrollment/payment workflows.

### Target Audiences
| Audience | Sector | Motion | Key Buyers |
|---------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Non-degree programs | Higher Ed | Cross-sell | University president, CIO, Director of Distance/Online Learning, Deans/Directors (decentralized campuses), Deputy/Pro Vice Chancellor (APAC) |
| Faculty training | Higher Ed | Cross-sell | Same as above |
| Educational Services | BizGov | Net-new | CLO, CTO, VP of Product |

### The Enemy
**Everything is different for non-degree programs and learners.** Traditional enrollment, payment, and completion tracking systems are built for degree programs — creating friction, missed enrollments, and lost revenue when applied to non-degree learners who expect consumer-grade digital experiences.

### Three Core Pain Points
1. **Adult learner expectations** — 73% of adult learners will abandon an institutional website that is not user-friendly (EAB, 2024). Non-degree learners want to discover, register, and pay instantly.
2. **Payment is different** — Traditional bursar systems aren't designed for course-by-course e-commerce payments required by non-degree programs.
3. **Enrollment processes are different** — Traditional enrollments sync via SIS; non-degree enrollments are typically managed outside the SIS, creating parallel workflows and duplicated effort.

### How Catalog Solves It
- Integrated storefront: makes it easy to explore, register, and pay in one experience
- Connects directly to Canvas for automated user creation and enrollment
- Supports both self-enrollment and SIS-based provisioning
- Eliminates redundant admin workflows and siloed enrollment systems

### Competitive Framing
Current-state alternatives that Catalog replaces:
- Self-managed marketing websites with disjointed enrollment/payment processes
- Separate tools for degree vs. non-degree programs (higher costs, complex admin)
- Manual workarounds that strain staff and create inconsistent learner experiences

### Elevator Pitch
"Catalog unifies the administration needed for non-degree and traditional programs without disrupting the learner experience of either. By integrating with Canvas, Catalog supports both self-enrollment and SIS-based provisioning, eliminating redundant workflows and siloed enrollment systems."

## What It Is
Canvas Career is a skills-based LMS solution for adult learners, built around Skillspace — an environment where learners actively build capability through course stacking and progression.

**TL;DR:** Unverified skills (Skillspace) — mirrors the reality of coursework and self-advocacy.

---

## The Enemy
**In an "easy button" learning market, convenience too often beats out quality.** AI has made information instantly accessible — learners and business leaders expect flexibility, speed, and immediate usefulness. Quality providers must now prove their value is worth the time and money, and prove they can deliver speed and personalization without diluting credibility.

---

| Sector | Motion | Key Buyers |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| BizGov – Educational Services (ICP 1) | Net-new | CLO, CTO, VP of Product |

| BizGov – Internal (ICP 3 & 4) | Net-new/Cross-sell | CLO, VP HR/People, Head of Enablement |
| Higher Ed | Cross-sell | Provost, VP of Academic Affairs |

---

## Value Pillars

**1. Deliver skills-aligned learning**
Canvas Career enables skills-based learning that connects programs directly to in-demand competencies, supporting both learner progression and employer-facing outcomes.

**2. Increase efficacy with AI**
AI-assisted content creation and progression tracking make it easier to design relevant, personalized learning at scale without sacrificing instructional quality.

**3. Measure impact**
Dashboards and outcomes data help organizations prove ROI on learning investments — connecting program completion to skills development and business performance.

- Two-thirds of adult learners say time flexibility is their top factor when selecting a program — Gallup 2024

## The Better Together Story

**Canvas Career is strongest when paired with:**

| Add | Why | Message |
|-----|-----|---------|
| **Canvas Catalog** | Front door: discoverability, enrollment, payments | "Catalog is the front door; Canvas Career is where skills are built." |
| **Parchment Digital Badges** | Proof: verifiable, portable credentials | "Canvas Career powers skill development; Digital Badges turns progress into verified proof." |

**Triple-play pitch (most compelling):**
- Catalog: Recruit, market, and enroll learners by showcasing skills and pathways
- Canvas Career: Deliver skill development at scale with AI-assisted content and dashboards
- Parchment Digital Badges: Turn demonstrated skills into portable, verifiable proof

**Combined one-liner:** "From enrollment to skills to verified outcomes: Instructure can help you create one connected pathway learners can follow and employers can trust."

## Competitive Framing
The enemy is not a specific competitor — it's the perception that AI chatbots and free content are "good enough." Canvas Career must win on quality, credibility, and proof of outcomes, not just convenience.
