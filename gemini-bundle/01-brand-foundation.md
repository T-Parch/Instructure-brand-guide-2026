## Tagline
**"Setting potential in motion."**
Use at the end of videos and presentations. Do not alter. Always paired with the Instructure logo in video contexts.

## Vision
"A future where education technology seamlessly amplifies the human potential to thrive in a perpetually changing world."

## Mission
"To help learners thrive in tomorrow's landscape by delivering a future-ready ecosystem."

## Brand Promise
Preparing educators and learners for upcoming challenges and opportunities.

## Brand Persona
Inspirational and human — friendly and familiar, yet forward-thinking. Communications evoke connection and collaboration, making engagement "fun" while leaving users "feeling supported in your success."

## AI use for brand decisions
The brand foundation should inform the **why** behind every content decision. If a piece of content doesn't support "setting potential in motion" or doesn't feel human and forward-thinking, it's off-brand.
