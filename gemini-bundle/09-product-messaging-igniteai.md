Used in context:

**Visual identity:** IgniteAI uses the **IgniteAI icon** and **purple gradients** as consistent visual indicators wherever AI is in use. This is the system's signal that an output may warrant additional human review.

## What It Is
Instructure IgniteAI is the secure, in-context AI solution for education. Embedded throughout the Instructure ecosystem (Canvas and Mastery), it streamlines content creation, grading and feedback, personalization, and data and insights.

**Headline:** Transform learning with secure, in-context AI

**Subhead:** Enhance educator impact and learner success with AI seamlessly woven into daily teaching, learning, and administration.

## Value Pillars

**1. Build Confidence with Transparency and Security**
Institutions need to know exactly what AI is doing with their data and their students' data.

- **AI "Nutrition Facts"** — Inline transparency into which AI models are in use, how data is handled, PII usage, and other salient information. Accessible via info icon within any IgniteAI feature.
- **Feature options** — Administrators control where and how AI is used within Instructure products. AI is only activated where it aligns with institutional policies.
- **Data privacy commitment** — By default, no institution or student data is used to train AI models.
- **Compliance coverage:** FERPA/COPPA (US), GDPR (EMEA), Privacy Act 1988 (AU), NZ Privacy Act 2020, Indonesia PDP Law 2022, Singapore PDPA 2012

**2. Guide AI Adoption Within Existing Workflows**
People are at different stages of AI readiness — IgniteAI meets them where they are.

- **Inline capabilities** built directly into Canvas and Mastery — no tab-switching, no new tool to learn
- **IgniteAI Agent for Canvas** (coming) — Persistent side panel for conversational AI assistance; can perform any action a user can, only when prompted
- **Partner AI integrations** available within Canvas: Google Gemini LTI, Anthropic Claude LTI, Grammarly, MagicSchool AI, Khanmigo Teacher Tools

**3. Drive Educator Efficiency and Learning Outcomes**
IgniteAI is purpose-built to reduce manual work while keeping educators in control.

- **AI-assisted content creation** — Leverages existing course materials and academic standards to accelerate content development while ensuring rigor and alignment
- **Grading and feedback assistance** — Speeds up review while educators retain full control over final scores
- **Personalization and accessibility** — Translations and tools that make creating inaccessible content harder; tailored learning experiences for every learner
- **Actionable insights** — Qualitative and quantitative data accessible to every instructor and leader; saves time and surfaces what matters

## Key Proof Points
- 59% of administrators express concerns about data privacy/security risks with AI models (+9pp year-over-year) — Ellucian AI Survey
- 40% of faculty are at the beginning of their AI literacy journey; only 17% identify as advanced or expert users
- 80% of faculty feel there is a lack of clarity on how AI can be applied in teaching within their institutions — Digital Education Council 2025
- Educators and admins agree #1 advantage of AI is completing tasks more efficiently
- Time and resources are the top barriers to faculty AI adoption

## What IgniteAI Is NOT
Do not position IgniteAI as a replacement for educator judgment. It is:
- An accelerator for content creation — not an author
- A grading assistant — not a grader
- A transparency layer — not a black box
- An in-context tool — not a separate app
