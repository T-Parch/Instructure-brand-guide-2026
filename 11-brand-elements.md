## Typography

### Primary: LL Circular (Lineto)
- Geometric sans-serif, designed by Laurenz Brunner (2008)
- 8 weights available; Instructure uses 4
- Characteristics: Rounded terminals, balanced proportions

### Alternate: Poppins
- Approved for web and Canva applications
- Use recommended weights only

| Element | Size | Notes |

## Photography

### Philosophy
Authentic, warm, human. Real people in real moments — not staged, not stock-cliché.

### Core Principles
- **Authenticity:** Unposed, spontaneous, genuine emotions
- **Natural expressions:** Genuine smiles, laughter, thoughtfulness, relaxed body language
- **Diversity:** Reflect the actual audience across identities and experiences
- **Natural light:** Warm, soft, inviting atmosphere
- **Subtle tech:** Technology supports human interaction — never the central subject

## Illustration

### Approach
Custom illustrations preferred over stock photography. Establishes unique brand identity.

**Inspiration:** Abbey Lossing, Andy J. Pizza

### Principles
1. **Style:** Flat, 2D. No gradients, no intricate shading.
2. **Color:** Thoughtful, balanced application. Mirrors brand palette hierarchy.
3. **Composition:** Distinct focal points. Simple, clear narratives. Subtle dot usage encouraged.
4. **Characters:** Diverse, relatable, natural emotions aligned with brand values.
5. **Avoid:** Stock visual clichés and overused tropes.
6. **Commissioning:** Provide full brief with guidelines, palettes, and style references.

## Iconography

### Philosophy
"Simple shapes, clear communication."

### Rules
- Flat, single-color
- Basic geometric shapes: circles, triangles, squares
- Function as **non-essential design elements**
- Any brand colorway is acceptable
